Personal profile

Zhi Li (李志)

Research Interests

Computing-in-Memory, Resistive Random Access Memory (RRAM), Hyperdimensional Computing (HDC), Deep Neural Network

Education

  • M.S. in Microelectronics and Solid State Electronics
  • Advisor. Prof. Ling Li /Jinshan Yue

Sep. 2018 - Jun. 2022: North University of China

  • B.S. in Microelectronic Science and Engineering

Publications
